Chocolate Shake

Description

This quick, easy choco shake recipe makes a creamy, luscious chocolate shake!

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops chocolate ice cream
  • 2 scoops chocolate ice cream
  • 1 cup milk

Directions

  1. Combine ice cream, drink mix powder, and milk in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve.
